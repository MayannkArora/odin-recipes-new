Lasagna

Lasgna

Lasagna is a famous dish originiating from Italy. A scrumptuous dish that pleases to food lovers all around the world.

Ingredients are as follows:

  • Pasta
  • Wheat flour
  • cheese

Here's how to prepare lasagna:

  1. Boil water for 2 minutes
  2. Add pasta and spices
  3. Wait until water has been boiled
  4. Add chese and a dash of wheat flour and stir till thick
  5. Enjoy your meal!
